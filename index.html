Executive Summary

read this page.

Recommended Actions

What to do.

Ordered by urgency and ROI. 90-day priorities are executable without new budget allocation.

01
Commission the reliability study.
Wakefield Research or equivalent. Publish findings as ad creative. Deploy to Reddit, YouTube Tech, and Best Buy review ecosystem simultaneously. Directly neutralizes the #1 purchase barrier with proof.
⚡ Start within 30 days
02
Build the FIFA retargeting layer.
15-second units — product name, screen size, price, "Shop now" — targeting audiences who engaged with FIFA content. Deploy Meta + YouTube within 48 hours of each CWC match. Converts existing asset investment into conversion.
⚡ Start within 14 days
03
Shift retail media from 8% to 22%.
Amazon Sponsored + A+ content refresh. Best Buy in-store digital. Walmart end-cap placement. This is where purchase decisions actually happen. TCL's retail dominance is the most addressable gap in the current media mix.
↗ 90-day ramp
04
Announce the Google TV fix.
Publish a firmware improvement roadmap. Offer OS choice (Fire TV / Roku) on select models. Forum users are making purchase decisions based on OS complaints. Silence is complicity. This is fixable and fast.

2025 Brand Narrative Direction

Built to last.
Built to impress.

The strategic choice is not about which campaign to run. It's about which story to own. Samsung owns innovation. LG owns picture quality. TCL owns sports-value. Sony owns cinema.

The only territory no one is defending is trust — the confidence that the TV you buy will still work in year four and five. Consumer Reports has handed Hisense the exact brief: make reliability the story, back it with data, and do it before anyone else does. The window is 12–18 months. The message is simple. Built to last. Built to impress.
