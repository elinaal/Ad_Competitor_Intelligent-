01 · Executive Summary

Three structural shifts redefined the competitive dynamic since late 2025. Q1 2026 data — new litigation, the TCL–Sony joint venture, Hisense shipment collapse in China — sharpens each shift. Each creates either an existential pressure or a window of opportunity.

Threat · #01
Samsung is redefining TV advertising — and TCL just bought Sony.
Samsung Performance TV launched March 2026 with 26× engagement. Q1 2026: TCL signed a 51% JV with Sony, gaining premium positioning + image-processing overnight. Two innovation-led offenses.

Hisense's core value proposition is actively weakening.
Q1 2026 shipments −13% YoY. China market collapse + 145% tariffs closing the gap to Samsung/LG. The ACR lawsuit adds a new trust risk. "Cheapest Chinese brand" no longer cheap, no longer safe.
Opportunity · #03
Reliability remains completely unclaimed territory.
Consumer Reports data is public. No competitor has responded. First-mover advantage window: 12–18 months.
